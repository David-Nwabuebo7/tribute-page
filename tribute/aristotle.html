Aristotle socrates onassis,Aristotelis onasis; 20 january 1906 -15 march 1975, [1]commonly called Ari or Arito onassis, was a Greek-argentine shipping magnate, who amassed the world's largest privately owned shipping fleet and was one of the world's richest and most famous men.
